Last Updated: April 8, 2026

1. Introduction

StreamFix Hub ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our technical support services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to provide you with technical support services. This includes:

  • Personal Information: Name, phone number, and email address provided when you contact us for support
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and streaming app version relevant to your support issue
  • Service Records: Details about the technical issues you experienced and the troubleshooting steps we performed
  • Communication Records: Transcripts or notes from your support session
  • Payment Information: Billing details related to our service fee (processed securely by third-party payment processors)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect exclusively to:

  • Provide technical support services you request
  • Diagnose and troubleshoot streaming service issues
  • Send you confirmation of service completion
  • Follow up on your support experience (quality assurance only)
  • Process payment for our services
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not use your information for marketing, advertising, or any other purpose without your explicit consent.

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise disclose your personal information to third parties.

We only share information with:

  • Payment Processors: Only to process your payment for our services
  • Service Providers: Only those who assist us in providing support (e.g., phone system providers) under strict confidentiality agreements
  • Legal Requirements: Only when required by law, court order, or government request

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services and support
  • Maintain service records (kept for 12 months from the date of service)
  • Comply with legal and financial obligations

After the retention period, your information is securely deleted or anonymized.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences

We use essential cookies only and do not use tracking cookies for advertising or behavioral targeting. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
