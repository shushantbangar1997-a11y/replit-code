How to Fix Common STARZ Problems

Updated April 2026  •  5 min read

STARZ delivers premium original series and movies, but activation failures, sign-in errors, and streaming quality problems are frequently reported. This guide walks through the most common STARZ issues and provides direct, actionable steps to fix them on any device.

🔑 How to Fix STARZ Activation Code Errors

Installing STARZ on a TV or streaming device shows an activation code you enter at starz.com/activate. Here's what to do when activation doesn't go through.

  • Open starz.com/activate on a phone or computer

    Make sure you are already signed in to your STARZ account in that browser before entering the code shown on your TV.

  • Enter the code quickly — it will expire

    Activation codes time out after a few minutes. If yours has expired, go back to the STARZ app on your TV, get a fresh code, and enter it immediately.

  • Clear your browser cache if the page stalls

    If starz.com/activate keeps loading, clear your browser cache or try a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, or Safari).

  • Restart the STARZ app and generate a new code

    Fully close STARZ on your streaming device, reopen it, and use the new code at starz.com/activate within a couple of minutes.

Tip If you subscribed to STARZ through Amazon Prime Video, Apple TV, or a cable provider, activation works differently — log in through your provider's app rather than directly via starz.com.

👤 How to Fix STARZ Login Problems

Can't sign in to STARZ? Wrong password, account not found, or a frozen login screen are all fixable. Work through these steps in order.

  • Reset your password at starz.com

    Go to starz.com, click "Sign In," then "Forgot Password?" Enter your email and follow the reset link. Check spam if the email doesn't arrive within a few minutes.

  • Check how you originally subscribed

    If you subscribed through Amazon, Apple, or a cable provider, you need to log in through that partner's system — not directly with a STARZ email and password.

  • Clear the STARZ app's cache on your device

    On Fire Stick or Android TV: Settings → Applications → Manage Installed Applications → STARZ → Clear Cache, then Clear Data. Reopen and sign in.

  • Uninstall and reinstall the STARZ app

    Remove STARZ entirely and re-download it. This clears any corrupted session data that may be preventing you from logging in.

Tip STARZ limits the number of simultaneous streams. If you're logged out, check whether another device on your account is actively streaming and sign out of all devices at starz.com/account.

📺 How to Set Up STARZ on Roku

STARZ is available on all supported Roku devices. Follow these steps to install and fix common Roku-specific issues.

  • Find STARZ in the Roku Channel Store

    Press Home on your remote, go to "Streaming Channels," then "Search Channels." Type "STARZ" and select "Add Channel."

  • Update Roku OS if STARZ won't install

    Settings → System → System Update → Check Now. Install updates and try adding STARZ again.

  • Launch STARZ and activate

    Open the channel — it will display an activation code. Go to starz.com/activate on another device, sign in, and enter the code.

  • Fix STARZ crashing on Roku

    Settings → System → System Restart. If crashes continue, remove STARZ from Roku and re-add it for a clean install.

Tip If STARZ was added as an Amazon channel, you may need to access it through the Amazon Video app on Roku rather than the standalone STARZ app.

🔥 How to Set Up STARZ on Amazon Fire Stick

STARZ works on Fire Stick 4K, Fire TV Stick Lite, and Fire TV Stick (2nd gen or later). Here's how to install and troubleshoot it.

  • Find STARZ in the Amazon Appstore

    Use the Search icon or Alexa and say "STARZ." Select the app and choose "Get" to install it.

  • Update Fire OS if the app crashes or won't install

    Settings → My Fire TV → About → Check for System Updates. Install pending updates, then try launching STARZ again.

  • Sign in or activate

    Open STARZ and sign in directly, or visit starz.com/activate on another device if an activation code appears on screen.

  • Fix errors by clearing cache and data

    Settings → Applications → Manage Installed Applications → STARZ → Clear Cache. If the issue continues, use "Clear Data" and sign in again.

Tip If you subscribe to STARZ as an add-on through Amazon Prime Video, open the Prime Video app instead of the standalone STARZ app — your content will all be there.

📶 How to Fix STARZ Buffering & Playback Issues

Buffering, pixelated video, or streams that randomly stop are almost always network or device performance issues. Work through these steps.

  • Check your internet speed

    STARZ needs at least 5 Mbps for HD streaming. Run a speed test at fast.com. If speed is too low, restart your router and modem.

  • Use a wired Ethernet connection

    Wi-Fi signal drops through walls and with distance. Connecting your streaming device directly to the router via Ethernet eliminates most wireless buffering.

  • Lower the video quality in STARZ

    In the STARZ app, go to Settings → Video Quality and reduce it to stop buffering on slower connections.

  • Restart your device and clear the STARZ app cache

    Unplug your streaming device for 30 seconds, then clear the STARZ app cache before relaunching. This frees memory that causes slow playback.

Tip If only STARZ buffers while other streaming apps work fine, check server status at downdetector.com/status/starz before troubleshooting your network.
